===========================
Presentations
===========================

Below a list of PROJ related presentations have been given at FOSS4G, the leading annual conference for free and open source geospatial software,
EUREF meetings or other events.

+ `Is here still where it was? Changes in terrestrial reference frames in R spatial packages <https://events.digital-research.academy/event/109/contributions/545/attachments/49/173/bivand_useR26_pres2.pdf>`_ by Roger Bivand, useR! 2026, Warsaw, Poland.
+ `PROJ is not only about projections <https://talks.osgeo.org/media/foss4g-europe-2026/submissions/SSCMFH/resources/PROJ-is-not-only-about-proje_wHvh0h8.pdf>`__ by Javier Jimenez Shaw, Pix4D, at `FOSS4G Europe 2026 <https://2026.europe.foss4g.org/>`_ in Timișoara, Romania.
+ `Introduction to Vertical Coordinate Systems <https://talks.osgeo.org/media/foss4g-europe-2024/submissions/B9V7SD/resources/Introduction_to_Vertical_Coordinate_Syst_SsXBI82.pdf>`__
  by Javier Jimenez Shaw, Pix4D, at `FOSS4G Europe 2024 <https://2024.europe.foss4g.org/>`_ in Tartu, Estonia. `Recording <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tELPb9G64zU>`__.
+ `Coordinate reference systems <https://rsbivand.github.io/csds_jan23/csds_crs_workshop_230131.html>`_ by Roger Bivand and Edzer Pebesma, at the University of Chicago Center for Spatial Data Science in January 2023. `Recording <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o0yMeb_UdE4>`__.
+ `Site Calibration with PROJ and WKT2 <https://talks.osgeo.org/media/foss4g-2023/submissions/EVESEF/resources/Site_Calibration_with_PROJ_and_WKT2_cSF0Urt.pdf>`__ by Javier Jimenez Shaw, Pix4D, at `FOSS4G 2023 <https://2023.foss4g.org/>`_ in Prizren, Kosovo. `Recording <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dvHB-oFR0kQ>`__.
+ `Introduction to Coordinate Systems <https://talks.osgeo.org/media/foss4g-2023/submissions/RNVJ3D/resources/Introduction_to_Coordinate_Systems__FGbr4Ar.pdf>`__
  by Javier Jimenez Shaw, Pix4D, at `FOSS4G 2023 <https://2023.foss4g.org/>`_ in Prizren, Kosovo. `Recording <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4OOGVIajt9Y>`__.
+ `PROJ, une librairie ouverte de transformation de coordonnées <https://cnig.gouv.fr/IMG/pdf/cnig_geopos_20221014_proj_-_une_librairie_ouverte_de_transformation_de_coordonnees.pdf>`_ (in French), Commission GeoPos, October 2022, by Even Rouault, Spatialys
+ `Transformations using PROJ <https://www.euref.eu/euref-documents/tutorials/transformations-using-proj>`_ by K. Evers, G. Valsson, P. Häkli, J. Lesparre et al., E. Brockmann and M. Clausen, at EUREF 2019, Tallin, Estonia.
+ `Why have CRS, projections and transformations changed? <https://r-spatial.github.io/evolution/CRS_projections_transformations.html>`_ by Roger Bivand
+ `Status report on PROJ <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9Ujsr2qgXUI>`_ by K. Evers, at FOSS4G-Europe 2018 in Guimarães.
+ `A history, status report, and outlook of Proj.4 <https://howardbutler.com/talks/2017-08-18-proj4>`_ in FOSS4G-NA 2017, by Howard Butler, Hobu Inc.
+ `Transformation pipelines for PROJ.4 <https://www.fig.net/resources/proceedings/fig_proceedings/fig2017/ppt/iss6b/ISS6B_evers_knudsen_9156_ppt.pdf>`_ at FIG Working Week, Helsinki, 2017, by K. Evers and T. Knudsen, Danish Agency for Data Supply and Efficiency. `Accompanying paper <https://www.fig.net/resources/proceedings/fig_proceedings/fig2017/papers/iss6b/ISS6B_evers_knudsen_9156.pdf>`__.
